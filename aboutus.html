About Us

Much like the writings of our founder, Edgar Allan Poe, Poe Distillery has accumulated a rich history of creating complex spirits. From the lighter flavors of Annabel Lee to the dark whiskey of the Tell-Tale Heart, our collection brings a drink for all of life's occasions. We also like to take the time to show others how we do it. Before you stop by for a visit, please check out the video to the left to find out more about the Whiskey making process to get an idea of how our alcohols are made.

And when you have a moment, please be sure to check out our favorite podcast below! It's called Basic Brewing Radio and each week they shed more light on the hobby of home brewing.
